MISSION

To mobilize and involve members of the Society who constitute the communities where specific projects shall be situated to participate by co-funding and volunteering services which may be pertinent to the growth and development of the Organization....

VISION

"To have a Society with Life Skills, Self-reliant and free from Poverty"...

Major Objective

To participate in the fight against ignorance, disease and poverty in our society by pooling, mobilizing and lobbing for resources locally and abroad.....

Specific Objectives

• To Provide Qualitative and Competitive Primary School Leavers through intensive teaching under a conducive Learning environment.
• To provide employment opportunities to the youth with educational and health skills.
• To demonstrate modern practices of environment management by demonstrating tree planting and proper Garbage disposal.
• Mobilize the community to fight HIV/AIDS through better moral behavioral practices. .
